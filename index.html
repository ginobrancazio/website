// ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm a tech nerd and game designer based in Cardiff. I specialise in building things that connect people.

I've spent 10 years designing and publishing board games in the tabletop industry, but now I'm combining my game design experience with my career in tech to build games and technology that bring people together.

When I'm not working in tech, you'll find me cosplaying as the greatest superhero of all time: Doc Ock

I'm also a member of a Historical European Martial Arts (HEMA) academy, focusing on traditional longsword techniques inspired by Renaissance master Achille Marozzo.

Currently interested in learning more about the video game world. Always open to interesting conversations and collaboration opportunities.
